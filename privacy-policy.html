Cookies and Their Use:

Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers transferred to the client's hard drives through their web browsers. This enables our systems to recognize the clients' browsers and ensure the storage of items in their Shopping Carts during visits. The Help portion of the toolbar on most browsers will be more helpful in explaining how to prevent the browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify the user when a new cookie is received, or how to disable cookies altogether. However, cookies allow you to take full advantage of the top features at our Site, and our company recommends that the client accepts them.

Second, we keep track of your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website. Your IP address is also used to gather broad demographic information about you, such as your location and Internet service provider. We may also collect combined information on how our users utilize the Site. This might include information regarding traffic patterns through the site and search queries. No IP address/log file information is tied to Personally Identifiable Information (PII).

Third, we log browser types, access times, URLs from which visitors visit our Site, and URLs viewed by visitors while on our Site. Except as otherwise stated in this Privacy Policy, we do not provide this information to third parties, except in combined form.

Our privacy statement does not cover the use of cookies by our partners, affiliates, tracking utility company, or service providers. We do not have access to or control over these cookies. Our partners, affiliates, tracking utility company, and service providers use session ID cookies to make navigating our Site easier.
